Loan Agreement Terms and Conditions

Please carefully read these terms and conditions before proceeding with your loan application.

1. Loan Amount and Interest

The loan amount you request will be subject to an interest rate based on the selected installment period:

  • 1 Month: 3%
  • 2 Months: 5%
  • 3 Months: 8%
  • 4 Months: 10%

2. Repayment Schedule

You agree to repay the loan amount according to the selected repayment schedule:

  • Daily Payments: Divided by 30 days for each month in the installment period.
  • Weekly Payments: Divided by 4 weeks for each month in the installment period.

3. Late Payment Fees

If payments are not made on time, a penalty fee of 5% of the outstanding balance will be charged for each overdue month.

4. Loan Default

Failure to repay the loan in full by the agreed term will result in loan default. The lender reserves the right to take necessary legal action to recover the outstanding amount.

5. Data Privacy

Your personal and financial information will be used solely for loan processing and will not be shared with unauthorized third parties.

6. Borrower Responsibilities

As the borrower, you agree to provide accurate information, adhere to the repayment schedule, and notify the lender of any changes in your financial situation.
